FAQ

Questions, answered

How do I buy a number?
Browse the marketplace, add a number to your cart, and complete checkout. Premium numbers can be purchased outright or leased-to-own, and our team can source a specific number on request.
What’s the difference between toll-free, local, and vanity numbers?
Toll-free numbers (800, 888, and similar) signal a national presence; local numbers carry a specific area code and local trust; vanity numbers spell a word or pattern for memorability. Many premium numbers are more than one of these at once.
Can I port my number to another carrier?
Yes. Numbers you acquire are yours to port to the carrier or platform of your choice, subject to standard porting validation.
Do you verify the numbers you sell?
Every listing is checked against the Number Graph for ownership, carrier, reputation, and validation before it is offered.
Can I list a number I already own?
Yes — contact our acquisition team to list premium numbers you own on the marketplace.
How do payment plans work?
Activate a number now and spread the cost over up to 36 months — interest-free, with a small down payment and no credit check. Shorter plans are fee-free; longer plans add a small service fee, and you can pay off early anytime.
How fast can a number be activated?
Most numbers activate within minutes. Numbers being ported in from another carrier can take longer while standard porting validation completes.
What is the Number Graph score on each listing?
Every listing is graded by the Number Graph across carrier, ownership, reputation, and validation signals, so you can see a number’s history and trust level before you buy.
Do you offer an API for developers?
Yes — lookup, validation, carrier, and provisioning APIs let you build number intelligence and activation directly into your application. See the Developer Platform.
What is your money-back policy?
Every listing is verified against the Number Graph before sale. If a number isn’t as described, reach out — our acquisition specialists will help make it right.

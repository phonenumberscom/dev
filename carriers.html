Carrier Compatibility

Port your number to any US or Canadian carrier

Every number on PhoneNumbers.com is fully portable. Activate it on our network today, then transfer it to any wireless, VoIP, or landline carrier whenever you're ready — no port-out fees, no lock-in, full portability guaranteed.

Porting notes for major carriers

AT&T

Wireless ports typically complete in a few hours to 1 business day. Prepaid lines may need the account number and transfer PIN from the AT&T app.

T-Mobile

Most transfers finish same-day once the port request is validated. Have your T-Mobile account number and 6-digit transfer PIN ready.

Verizon

Ports usually complete within 24 hours for wireless and 2–5 business days for Fios landlines. Number Lock must be disabled before porting.

Sprint

Legacy Sprint numbers port through the T-Mobile network. Same-day completion is typical for active, paid-up accounts.

Cox

Landline and digital voice ports take 3–7 business days. Keep the line active until the port completes to avoid losing the number.

How porting works

Five steps, no dead air

  1. Buy your number — it activates on our licensed US carrier network within minutes.
  2. Use it immediately — forward calls, park it, or connect service while you decide where it lives.
  3. Request the port from your new carrier — give them the account number and transfer PIN from your dashboard.
  4. Carriers validate and schedule — the receiving carrier confirms the details with us automatically.
  5. Your number goes live on the new carrier — the switchover is seamless, with no gap in service.

Before you port — checklist

  • Your account number (in your PhoneNumbers.com dashboard)
  • Your transfer PIN (generated in the dashboard)
  • Billing name matches exactly on both accounts
  • Keep the number active — never cancel first; porting moves the number, cancelling releases it

Porting FAQ

How long does it take to port a number?

Local numbers typically port in 1–3 business days. Toll-free numbers usually complete in 1–2 business days. Wireless-to-wireless transfers are often same-day.

Will my number stay active during the transfer?

Yes. Your number keeps working on our network until the moment the new carrier activates it — there is no dead period.

Can I port my number to a VoIP service like RingCentral or Google Voice?

Yes. Every number we sell can be ported to any US or Canadian VoIP provider, or connected directly through call forwarding while you decide.

Does it cost anything to port my number out?

We never charge a port-out fee. Your receiving carrier may have its own activation fees — check with them before starting the transfer.

What if my carrier isn't listed here?

This list covers common carriers, but any US or Canadian wireless, VoIP, or landline carrier can receive your number. If you can name it, we can port to it.

Can I port a number I just bought?

Yes. Numbers are portable as soon as they're activated on our network — typically within minutes of purchase. There is no required holding period.

What can make a port fail?

The usual causes are a mismatched account name, a wrong account number or transfer PIN, or an inactive line. Double-check those details from your dashboard before submitting the request to your new carrier.

Do I lose my number if the port fails?

No. A failed port simply leaves the number where it is — active on our network. Fix the detail that caused the rejection and resubmit; nothing is lost.

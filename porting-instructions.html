Porting Directory

Carrier Porting Instructions

Everything you need to move a number out of 41 US and Canadian carriers — where to find your account number, how to get the transfer PIN, expected timing, and the mistakes that get ports rejected.

AT&T Wireless Full guide →WirelessWireless often same day to 1 business day

Account number

Bill or myAT&T account; commonly upper-right of bill

Transfer PIN

Number Transfer PIN required for wireless; request close to port time

Port-out steps

Request NTP in myAT&T or by dialing *PORT/*7678; give account #, NTP, billing ZIP/name/address to new carrier

Common rejection risks

Wrong NTP, inactive/suspended line, mismatch on account name/address/ZIP, device finance issues not port blockers but can create billing risk

Verizon WirelessWirelessWireless often same day to 1 business day

Account number

My Verizon or bill

Transfer PIN

Number Transfer PIN required; Number Lock must be off

Port-out steps

Turn off Number Lock if enabled; generate Number Transfer PIN in My Verizon or #PORT; provide acct # + PIN

Common rejection risks

Number Lock on, wrong transfer PIN, wrong account owner info, inactive line
